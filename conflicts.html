Conflicts

What are they and how I can fix them?

What's a conflict?

And why are you alerting me on this?

A conflict between extensions arise when you have themes or extensions that remove things that XKit needs to operate,
have different extensions that try to do the same thing, or have different versions XKit.

But why?
I've seen so many people complaining that XKit was not working correctly, only for them to say that it started working when they've disabled or removed the other dashboard themes or other Tumblr extensions they have installed. Some extensions, especially dashboard themes, either remove items from the page, or have problematic or buggy code that can prevent XKit from operating correctly.
 

What should I do?

In order for XKit to work correctly, it is highly recommended (or mandatory, if you have different versions of XKit installed) to remove extensions. While removing other extensions are optional, I recommend removing them, especially if you run into problems installing or using XKit.
 

How can I remove extensions?

It's really easy.


Chrome
  1. Click on the "three lines" button on top right
  2. Select Settings menu option
  3. Select Extensions from the left menu
  4. Click on the trash button and click Remove
Apple Safari
  1. Click on the Safari menu on menu bar
  2. Click on Preferences
  3. Click on Extensions
  4. Click on any extension you want to remove and click Uninstall or uncheck the Enable box
Firefox
  • Follow the official instructions available here.
