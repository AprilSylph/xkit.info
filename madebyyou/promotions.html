Thank you.

Whenever I see a post recommending XKit, it makes me happy. Whenever I see a post where an XKit user is helping a new-comer, it makes me even happier. Thank you for all your help, thanks for sharing XKit and helping me reach new people.
 

Bigger, better, faster, stronger

Why does more users mean a better XKit?
Simple: more bug reports means less bugs. More donations means less downtime on XIM and XKit servers. More users means more diversity, and from diversity comes new ideas and suggestions. All of these things make XKit faster, more stable and less buggier, and have more features for you to like and use.
 

Again, thank you.

For everyone who recommended XKit to their followers or friends, put badges on their blogs and/or helped other XKit users, I can not thank you enough. It's thanks to you XKit is so big, reaching more users, and becoming better every day.
