Notes

Small bits of information from the XKit Guy

How to Upgrade XKit

The "framework" part of XKit is not automatically updated by itself.
On Chrome, it should be updated by the browser, but it might fail for some reasons,
and on Firefox, Safari and Opera, you need to update it yourself.

Checking XKit Version

Click on the XKit Control Panel icon, then click on About + Support tab.
The version will be displayed on the top of the panel.

Google Chrome (and derivatives)

Click on the "options" button () on the top right corner of the Chrome window then select Tools > Extensions.
Click on the "Developer Mode" checkbox to make sure it's checked, then click on Update All Extensions button.
If the version of XKit changes, you've successfully updated XKit.

If the version does not change, remove XKit and re-download it from the download page.

Firefox, Safari and Opera

Just go to the download page and re-install XKit. Your settings will be preserved and XKit will be up to date.
You might need to restart your browser afterwards.
