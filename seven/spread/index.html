Spread XKit

Sharing is good.

Badges! Badges! Badges!

If you find XKit useful, let other people enjoy its usefulness too!
All badges sit on the bottom-right corner of the screen, faded until you hover over them so they would not distract your followers.
 

How to use these?

If your theme supports Custom HTML / Descriptions
If your theme supports it, you can just copy and paste it on your blogs description under the Customize page.

If the method above didn't work
If it does not, and if you can edit the HTML of the theme you use, just copy and paste them into the HTML, after the "<body>" tag.
